Introduction
Legend of LuWa is a real-time fighting adventure game.
In an ancient Chinese village, you will play LuWa, a teenager warrior whose destiny is to return peace to your homeland. Along the long and complex journey, you need both dexterity and intelligence to defeat monsters, discover treasures, save villagers, go through mazes, solve puzzles and much more! Finally, you will enter Xuan Tower, defeat Monster WuXing and save Green Angel.
system requirements;
System requirements
The suggested software/hardware for running this game is:
Windows98/2000/Me or Windows95+DirectX;
Personal Computer with Pentium CPU or better;
22M hard disk space;
Sound card (optional);
Gamepad or joystick (optional).

Install, run and uninstall the game
Install the game
If your CD-ROM drive is set to Auto-Play mode, the installation process will automatically start when the the game CD is inserted into the CD-ROM drive. Otherwise, double click setup.exe on the CD to start installation. Follow the instructions to proceed. Installation usually takes less than one minute.
Run the game
To start the game, choose 
Start-Program-Legend of LuWa-Legend of LuWa
, or if you see file Luwa.exe, double click the file. You may start a new game or load one of three saved progresses. Playing for the first time, you should always choose 
New Game
During the game running, follwing function keys are available: F1 key to get Help. F4 key to switch between windows mode and full screen mode. Alt+Tab to switch to other programs. Alt+F4 to quit the game.

How to play with keyboard
To play this game, you need four arrow keys and D key, F key, Esc key and space bar.
During fighting
Use arrow keys to walk or 
. D to wave sword. F to fire 
bullets
 or 
bombs
. Space to cast Blood Magic. Esc to enter menu.
Note: D, F, and space keys are only effective when you obtain the relative weapon or magic.
During talking
Use arrow keys to start talking by walking towards a person. D or space to continue talking. F or Esc to abort.
In menu
Use arrow keys to change your choices. D or space to make your choice. F or Esc to exit menu.

Getting started
You, LuWa, were in your home when a new game starts.
Walk towards a drawer to get 20 coins and leave your house.
Try to enter every house and talk to every villager you see. Obtain the Practise Sword by talking to an old man sitting in the graveyard.
After obtaining the sword, you may leave the village. Kill the monsters by waving your sword (pressing D on keyboard). You may collect some gold coins and other good stuff after killing the monsters.
Your immediate tasks include: learn to fly darts, kill the wolf in a cave and get a life container, enter the first dungeon and kill Big Monkey.

Tips
A sound 
ding
 means the attack is ineffective. You have to change the sword, bullet, or kungfu.
Some monsters are not vulnerable when flying or jumping. You have to wait till it lands.
There are different doors inside the dungeons. Some are open only when all the monsters in the room are perished (indicated by ??? when you try to open without killing all the monsters). Some are one-way doors (indicated by !!! when you try to enter from the wrong way).
There are different walls in the dungeons. Some are fake. Some are invisible.

Weapon and armor
On the weapons and armors submenu, you may change your sword, your bullets or bombs and your armor. Make sure you equip yourself with the new weapon and armor after you obtain them.
Your most important weapon is your sword. The swords are:
Practise Sword: attacking power 6, no 
sword bullets
Bravery Sword: attacking power 15, no sword bullets.
Dragon Sword: attacking power 30, 3 sword bullets.
Fire Sword: attacking power 60, 5 sword bullets.
Xuan Sword: attacking power 100, 3 sword bullets.
